STORE POLICIES

At Kathmandu Organics, we want to give our customers the most enjoyable shopping experience, one that will keep them coming back to our store time and time again. That’s why we believe that our store policies should be fair, clear and transparent. Below you’ll find a list of all our policies. If you can’t find the information you’re looking for - please don’t hesitate to contact us today!

About Our Policy

RETURNS

We are pleased to welcome you as a new customer. We always try to ensure that our products meet the expectations and quality you are looking for. But sometimes things go wrong or not as expected.


24 -hour return policy - if you have any issues with perishable and frozen products like vegetables, fruits, cheese, milk, cheese, cakes, chocolates, meat, fish, khuwa, cheese, we should be informed within 24 hours about it. We are sorry to inform you that complaints registered after 24 hours won't be addressed.


7 -days return policy - We have a 7-days return policy for groceries and other perishable items. We are sorry to inform you that complaints registered after 7-days won't be addressed.


To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Customers should present the bill as proof of purchase to return the product.


Return charges will be equivalent to the delivery service charges.


REFUNDS-

no physical damages or tampering are accepted

Once your return is received and inspected, we will get in touch with you. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.


If your refund is approved, our logistics team member will contact you to arrange a refund. We do not accept refunds without the original receipt

Delivery charges are non-refundable.



REPLACE-

applicable for same products

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged.



*Delivery charges are applicable in all conditions
