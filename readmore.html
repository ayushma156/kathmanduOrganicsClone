The Story of
Kathmandu Organics



Kathmandu Organics has continually featured a variety of high-quality and affordable products since day one. We are a curated online marketplace for local, organic and handmade products of Nepal. We collaborate with farmers, cooperatives and entrepreneurs to create sustainable value-chain for their products. So far, we have completed over 70,000 deliveries and worked with groups from 40 districts of Nepal since 2017


Our passion for excellence has driven us from the beginning and continues to drive us into the future. The team at Kathmandu Organics knows that every product counts, and strives to make the entire shopping experience as rewarding and fun as possible.
