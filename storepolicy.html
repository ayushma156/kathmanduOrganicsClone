STORE POLICIES

At Kathmandu Organics, we want to give our customers the most enjoyable shopping experience, one that will keep them coming back to our store time and time again. That’s why we believe that our store policies should be fair, clear and transparent. Below you’ll find a list of all our policies. If you can’t find the information you’re looking for - please don’t hesitate to contact us today!

Delivery & Shipping Policy

We are trying to deliver our products to all over Nepal.

Deliveries are done from Sunday – Friday.

Delivery charges and shipping time are as follows

  • a) Inside RingRoad - Rs. 60 - within 1 business day
  • b) Outside Ring Road - Rs.100 - within 2 business days
  • c) Bhaktapur - Rs.150 - within 2 business days
  • d) Outside Kathmandu Valley - Rs.200 or more - within 3 days

*There is no minimum order requirement.

FREE DELIVERY

  • a) Inside RingRoad - for orders worth Rs.1000 or more
  • b) Outside Ring Road - for orders worth Rs.2000 or more
  • c) Bhaktapur - for orders worth Rs.2000 or more
  • d) Outside Kathmandu Valley - Rs.200 or more - within 3 days
